COVID19 Impact - Bergen County

The first positive COVID19 case was reported on 3/5, and first COVID19 related death was reported after 5 days later on 3/10. On 5/10, the cumulative COVID19 positive case count was 16,929. On 5/10, the cumulative death count was 1,355. Bergen County population is 932,202.

Summary,
Looking at the death to positive COVID19 case ratio, for every 200 positive cases, roughly 16 people died. For every 20,000 people in the county, roughly 29 people died due to virus. For every 2,000 people in the county, roughly 36 people reported positive symptons.
