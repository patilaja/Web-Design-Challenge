COVID19-NJ Status Summary- May 10, 2020
The purpose of this project was to analyze how COVID-19 has impacted New Jersey's counties and to understand the trend which can lead to help flattening the curve. New Jersey is among one of the states that has been hit the hardest. It is crucial to stay informed on where the virus is spreading to understand how it could affect families, commerce, and travel.

The COVID-19 case and death rate per county must take into account the population of each count. Four counties were chosen for this study, two of them with the highest population and two of them with the lowest population.

To accomplish this analysis, data was obtained from covid19.nj.gov and usafacts.org to gather data on the 21 counties.

After assembling the datasets, Matplotlib and Seaborn were used to plot various aspects of COVID-19 positive cases vs death count for the most current data. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis as well as explanations and descriptions of any trends.
